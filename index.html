Who we are

Geometry Lap is a collaborative group of experts in algorithm design, optimization, computational geometry, and applied AI for 3D environments.

Our mission is to make advanced geometry methods accessible for real-world visualization, design automation, and robotics-oriented workflows.

What we build

  • Geometry-processing tools for modeling, analysis, and automation
  • Certified and robust numerical methods for complex geometric structures
  • 3D pipeline utilities for planning, simulation, and visualization tasks
  • Practical integrations that bridge research-grade math and production software

Computational Geometry

Algorithms for topology-aware shape processing, curves, surfaces, and polyhedral structures.

Optimization + AI

Constraint-based generation and optimization for spatial layouts and design decisions.

3D Tooling

Blender-centered tools that accelerate modeling, geometry exploration, and iteration.

Featured Product: Mesh Encyclopedia

Mesh Encyclopedia is a Blender add-on that provides a continuously updated library of 500+ production-ready meshes and curves.

  • Large categorized set of polyhedra, parametric, implicit, and TPMS forms
  • Directly available inside the Blender 3D Viewport workflow
  • Useful for design, shading, rendering, animation, and technical exploration
